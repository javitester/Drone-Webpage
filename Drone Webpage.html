About Us

Our project operates at the intersection of the humanitarian aid sector and the aerospace industry, leveraging innovative drone technology to deliver essential supplies in crisis zones. By focusing on sustainability, the project transforms recycled aircraft materials into drones capable of efficiently addressing logistical challenges in remote and conflict-affected areas.

Our Partners

We are proud to collaborate with renowned organizations and companies to bring innovative solutions to humanitarian challenges. Our partnerships with ONGs like the Red Cross allow us to deliver aid to the most vulnerable populations in times of crisis.

In addition, we work with aerospace industry leaders such as Airbus and Boeing, refurbishing aircraft materials to create sustainable and efficient drones. This collaboration enables us to recycle and repurpose valuable resources, further enhancing the impact of our mission.
